Projects

"Biocharge" Energy Harvesting Device
Engineering Senior Design

Senior Design Capstone project with the goal of developing a wearable biomechanical energy harvesting device to charge an iPhone with a net zero carbon footprint. Narrowed down a list of biomechanical charging mechanisms through materials processing to the compression of double-layered lead zirconate titanate crystal as an additive component of footwear. Sketched, designed, and reiterated prototypes using Solidworks, PLC Systems, and 3-D printed support structures. Successfully charged an iPhone 14 as part of a Senior Design Expo.

Electrosurgery Pen Design
Medtronic — Surgical Innovations Department

Created three training documents aimed at improving electrosurgery patient outcomes, impacting over 100 million surgical procedures worldwide. I also partnered with the R&D Department to develop three new design initiatives for pre-product development, conducting a double-blinded study in which I interviewed 10 surgeons and nurses to incorporate direct user feedback into future designs. Additionally, I tested three electrosurgery modes — cut, cauterize, and Valleylab™ Mode — in a laboratory setting alongside the R&D Department, applying the insights gained to inform new design directions.

Predictive Analytics Dashboard
Maritime Security Center

Developed a predictive analytics dashboard with a multidisciplinary team of amazing engineers to provide insight on over 10 years of coastal incident data for the US Coast Guard. Applied statistical aggregation strategies and seasonality systems using Python to create an adaptable polar chart used to predict coastal incidents based on time of year. Presented a Tableau-based dashboard for the US Coast Guard to adopt new technologies and better allocate manpower for its 40,000+ active personnel.

Priority Meter Allocation Script
Con Edison AMI Project

Developed an algorithm to identify over 10,0000 priority meter banks for allocation to ConEdison technicians as part of the Advanced Meter Infrastructure (AMI) Project (2016-2021). Created an excel-based script that sorted Manhattan meter banks based on categorical and numerical properties of importance. Connected local script to ConEdison server database to provide reliable real time meter identification. Provided identified meter banks to technicians in densely populated areas of Manhattan.
